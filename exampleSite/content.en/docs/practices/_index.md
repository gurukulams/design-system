The **Practices Module** is the interactive heartbeat of the Gurukulams platform. Based on the philosophy that **Questions are the source of Knowledge**, this module moves beyond passive reading to active participation. It provides a diverse suite of assessment types designed to challenge learners, reinforce core concepts, and provide immediate feedback.


## Interactive Assessment Types

To ensure high learner engagement, we support various question formats:

* **Multiple Choice Questions (MCQ):** Standard single-response questions to test factual recall and conceptual understanding.
* **True or False:** Quick-fire checks to validate fundamental principles.
* **Code-Based Exercises:** Interactive snippets where learners must identify errors or predict the output of a program.
* **Multi-Select:** Complex scenarios requiring the identification of multiple correct solutions.

## Why Practice Matters

1.  **Active Recall:** Transitioning from "knowing" to "doing" helps cement information in long-term memory.
2.  **Immediate Validation:** Learners receive instant results, allowing them to correct misunderstandings in real-time.
3.  **Engagement:** Interactive elements break the monotony of text-heavy documentation, keeping the learning process dynamic.
