# Choose the Best
Identifying the most accurate answer.

---

## Why this format?
It tests depth of understanding rather than simple recall.

---

## Example
Which is the most efficient sorting algorithm for small datasets?
* A) Merge Sort
* B) Quick Sort
* C) Insertion Sort
